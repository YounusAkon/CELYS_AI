Account Deletion

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your CELYS AI account at any time directly from the app:

  1. Open the app and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to My Profile.
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the screen and tap Delete Account, located below the Logout button.
  4. Review the confirmation message and tap Delete to confirm.

What Happens When You Delete Your Account

  • Your account is deactivated and you are immediately signed out of all devices.
  • Any active subscription is cancelled.
  • You will no longer be able to sign in using your existing email and password.
  • Your chat messages, mood check-ins, and other account data are retained but are no longer accessible to you.

If you create a new account later using the same email address, your name, password, profile photo, and subscription plan will be reset, but chat and mood check-in history previously associated with that email may remain linked to the new account.

To request permanent erasure of all data associated with your account, contact us at support@celysai.com.
